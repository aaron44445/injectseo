# The 5-Step SEO Checklist Every Aesthetic Practice Needs in 2026

SEO isn't magic. It's a checklist. If you're a med spa owner in 2026, skip these 5 steps at your own peril.

## ✅ Step 1: Claim & Optimize Your Google Business Profile (GBP)

Your GBP is more important than your website for local searches.
* **NAP Consistency:** Ensure Name, Address, and Phone match *exactly* everywhere online.
* **Categories:** Use "Medical Spa" as primary, plus "Botox Clinic," "Dermal Filler Clinic," etc.
* **Photos:** Upload 5-10 new photos monthly (treatments, team, office).
* **Posts:** Post weekly updates (offers, new services, before/afters).

## ✅ Step 2: Implement Medical Schema Markup

Google needs to understand you're a *medical* practice, not just a spa.
* **LocalBusiness** + **MedicalBusiness** schema.
* **Physician** schema for your doctors.
* **Service** schema for every treatment (Botox, Fillers, Lasers).
* **Review** schema to display stars in search results.

## ✅ Step 3: Optimize for "Near Me" & City Keywords

Patients search "Botox near me" or "Med Spa in [City]."
* **Title Tags:** "Best Med Spa in [City] | Botox & Fillers | [Practice Name]"
* **H1 Tags:** Include your city and primary service.
* **Content:** Mention your city and neighborhoods naturally throughout the page.
* **Location Pages:** If you serve multiple cities, create a dedicated page for each.

## ✅ Step 4: Build High-Quality Backlinks

Links from other sites tell Google you're authoritative.
* **Local Directories:** Chamber of Commerce, Healthgrades, RealSelf.
* **Industry Sites:** MedSpaInsider, Aesthetic Medical Professionals.
* **Local News:** Sponsor a local event and get a link from the news site.
* **Guest Posts:** Write for local health blogs.

## ✅ Step 5: Mobile-First & Speed Optimized

60%+ of your patients search on mobile.
* **Core Web Vitals:** LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1.
* **Mobile Design:** Buttons must be thumb-friendly. Forms must be simple.
* **Click-to-Call:** Ensure your phone number is clickable on mobile.

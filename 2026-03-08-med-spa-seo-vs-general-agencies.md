## The 3 Critical Differences

Google treats medical content as **YMYL**. One wrong word, and you get penalized.
* **Generic Agency:** Writes "Get Botox now!" (Too salesy, risky).
* **Med Spa Specialist:** Writes "Learn about Botox benefits and safety" (Educational, compliant).

**The Risk:** Generic agencies get your site flagged for "medical misinformation," killing your rankings instantly.

### 2. Hyper-Local Intent

Med spa patients don't search "Botox." They search "Botox in [City]" or "Med Spa near me."
* **Generic Agency:** Targets national keywords ("Best Botox").
* **Med Spa Specialist:** Targets local intent ("Botox in Miami," "Dermal Fillers in Dallas").

**The Result:** You're competing with everyone in the world instead of just the 5 practices within 5 miles.

"Botox" and "Dermal Fillers" are not the same.
* **Generic Agency:** Treats them as "cosmetic services."
* **Med Spa Specialist:** Understands the difference in patient intent, recovery time, and pricing models.
